I'm a big fan of communicating science to astronomy societies and school groups! I have experience delivering outreach both virtually and in-person, through presentations and more interactive sessions. I typically communicate about exoplanets, exoplanet detection, and planet formation, although I enjoy discussing all things science! I've also been involved with recording videos for the the UK Research Institute's Instagram page (discussing the Three Body Problem show, explaining planet formation, "fun fact Friday", and star signs).

Astrobites

I write regularly for the online blog Astrobites, where I summarise papers uploaded to Arxiv into a more accessible format, aimed at undergraduate students with an interest in astronomy. I try to create an illustration to accompany each article I write; you can find these on my science graphics page.

  • Cosmic Copycat - The First Detection of Water Ice in an Exo-Kuiper Belt - first detection of water ice in an exo-Kuiper Belt with JWST.
  • More Than a Name - Why Women in Physics Deserve Context - interview with Dr Claire Davies about the presentation of women in STEM.
  • Water, Water, Everywhere - And Not a Drop to Photodissociate! - detection of water vapour in PDS 70 with JWST.
