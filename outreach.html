I'm a big fan of communicating science to astronomy societies and school groups! I have experience delivering outreach both virtually and in-person, through presentations and more interactive sessions. I typically communicate about exoplanets, exoplanet detection, and planet formation, although I enjoy discussing all things science! I've also been involved with recording videos for the the UK Research Institute's Instagram page (discussing the Three Body Problem show, explaining planet formation, "fun fact Friday", and star signs).
